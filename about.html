About Us

About The School

Founded in April 1994 and christened after the great Saint Soldier Guru Gobind Singh Ji, St. Soldier International School is an English medium Co-educational Senior Secondary Institution. It is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi (Affiliation Code: 1630173). The school occupies a place of pride among the educational institutions of Mohali and is managed by St. Soldier Educational Society.
The main objective of this institution is to create an environment conducive to learning for the students and provide opportunities for them to realize their hidden potential. The school lays emphasis on character building and development of individual aptitude. The curriculum has been designed to develop a child physically, intellectually and emotionally so that he/ she emerges as a responsible and well balanced citizen. For this purpose the school provides a team of highly qualified, experienced and dedicated teachers.
